Graphic design work

Spring 2020 • ARTD 217 (Graphic Design for Non-Majors)

#School #Illustrator #InDesign #Procreate

Description

Posters created mainly in Adobe Illustrator for an intro to graphic design course.

PROMPTS

▶ A piece fashioned in the shape of one of my initials (B) that summarizes various aspects of my life and personality.

▶ An infographic paying homage to a graphic designer, with illustrations inspired by their style. My chosen designer was Aries Moross.

▶ A poster that depicts, supports or criticizes, and generally invites discussion on a constitutional amendment. My chosen amendment was the Fifteenth Amendment, and this piece aimed to highlight how voter suppression and disenfranchisement remain pressing issues deeply embedded in today's political landscape.
